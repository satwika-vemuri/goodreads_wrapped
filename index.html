Welcome to Goodreads Wrapped!

Our names are Malina Freeman, Avni Hulyalkar and Satwika Vemuri. All three of us are freshmen in Viterbi who love to read! As participants in AthenaHacks, all three of us wanted to get experience with data processing using CSV files and ended up making this tool! Go to Goodreads (web version!), My Books, and export your library. Then, upload it to our website, filter by year, and see a whole range of personalized information!
